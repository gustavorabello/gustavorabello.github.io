High Performance System for Multiphase Flow Simulation in Complex Geometry

Overview

The numerical simulation is a essential tool to troubleshoot multiphase flows found in many physico-chemical processes, such as the oil and gas extraction and processing, filling of hydroelectric reservoirs, drug-eluting transport in biological systems and cooling of high technology electronic components, all of them of utmost importance for achieving technology and development to the State of Rio de Janeiro.

Details

It is in this scope that the present project is part of, aimed at improving and adding new features on a high perfor- mance platform of multiphase flow simulation in geometries with high level of complexity. This tool is able to predict in details physical phenomena hard to be observed through Experimental and analytical reproduction, such as those found in biological systems, more specifically in the transport substances that can cause total or partial blockage of arteries undergoing stenting for CAD treatment and cooling of electronic by analysis of multiphase flows in parallel channels. Indicators of performance that will be used in the project are based on publications produced by the professor/researcher and subject to assessments of the scientific community.
