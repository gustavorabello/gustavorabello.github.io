Professorship

Since July 2014 I am adjoint professor of fluid dynamics at the Mechanical Department of the State University of Rio de Janeiro (UERJ) located at Maracana neighbourhood in the city of Rio de Janeiro. I dedicate my work time in teaching and research. Currently I’m teaching Fundamentals of Heat and Transfer I and II for undergraduate students and Multiphase Flows for graduate students (master and doctoral levels). Moreover I am the coordinator of the Seminars Sections of the Graduate Program in Mechanical Engineering of PPG-EM/UERJ.

I am member of the International Conference on Multiphase Flows (ICMF) organizing committee of the next conference to be held in Rio de Janeiro in May 2019.
