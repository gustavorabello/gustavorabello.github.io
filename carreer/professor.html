Professorship

Posted: Mon, 14 Jul 2014

From July 2014 to February 2019 I wasm adjoint professor of fluid dynamics at the Mechanical Department of the State University of Rio de Janeiro (UERJ) located at Maracana neighbourhood in the city of Rio de Janeiro. I dedicated my work time in teaching and research. I teached Fundamentals of Heat and Transfer I and II for undergraduate students and Multiphase Flows for graduate students (master and doctoral levels). Moreover I was the coordinator of the Seminars Sections of the Graduate Program in Mechanical Engineering of PPG-EM/UERJ.
